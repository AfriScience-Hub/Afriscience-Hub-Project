13. USER EXPERIENCE RULES

The dashboard should:

Maintain the same design language as the public website.

Avoid complex admin-style layouts.

Use clear spacing and structured cards.

Keep the interface intuitive for both technical and non-technical users.

14. DESIGN STYLE

Use:

Clean cards
Light backgrounds
Strong typography
Minimal shadows

Maintain a professional ecosystem platform feel.

Avoid overly playful design.

💡 One suggestion I’d give you as a product designer:

Your dashboard should also include a Profile Completion Progress Bar like:

"Profile Completion: 60%"

This encourages users to finish their listings, which increases platform quality.
